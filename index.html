About Us

OBJECTIVE

The aim of making this project to be in my portfolio is majorly that I love to travel. Recently, I went to Palampur, and it is actually a hidden gem in the foothills of the Himalayas. I loved most things about it. So, I decided that if some topic is to be decided for my project, it gotta be something related to what I like. Also, underrated places are not majorly talked about. Most websites showcase both overrated, normal and underrated destinations. So, this website will focus on only the ones which are overlooked by most visitors. Happy journey!

VISION

I will only do only good things with this website further on. I will make the list longer as soon as I get to know more places. If someone emails or texts me that they want some specific place to be added, I will add it, as long as it is not very explored. This will forever remain an informational website and will serve its basic purpose.

THE TEAM

I am currently the only one who did everything on this website. Hi, I’m Ashneet, an individual who likes exploration and creativity. So, I decided to combine both. Basically, this is the northern part of India since that’s where I am from. I will venture further now. Wish me a happy journey too!
